About Us

OUR MISSION

To be a strong pillar in the fight against poverty among individuals living in underdeveloped nations.

OUR VISION

Mobilizing the Unreached and showing them the potential of their knowledge, that they may be driven to improve their living standards..

Read More



How we Work

As our name suggests, we reach out to the less priviledged and the miniority in society and seek to help empower them consequentially making them productive individuals of the community.

Education is key in one`s live so we made it our mission to educate the orphans and less priviledged children through Rush Schools.

We set up Vocational training centers to help those who could not be able to attain basic education to obtain skills to help them earn a living

A good community warrants a prosperous people so we set up a number of community projects and initiatives inorder to enhance the people's productivity
